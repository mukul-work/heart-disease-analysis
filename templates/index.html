Heart Disease Risk Analysis

This project explores relationships between key health indicators and heart disease outcomes using interactive visual analytics.

View Dashboard View Data Story

Project Overview

The analysis focuses on identifying patterns between clinical attributes such as cholesterol levels, age, and blood pressure with heart disease occurrence. Tableau dashboards were used to visualize trends and explore risk factors.

Key Insights

  • Higher age groups show significantly greater heart disease prevalence
  • Elevated cholesterol correlates with increased risk
  • Blood pressure patterns vary strongly across age categories
